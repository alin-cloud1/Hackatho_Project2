Hackathon Problem Statement: The Fall
of Kodu Kuddus

The Background Story

In the peaceful realm of Class 7, Section B, a dark era has begun. Meet Kodu Kuddus, a boy
whose ego is much larger than his academic capabilities. Kodu recently won the Class Captain
election by promising free jhalmuri to everyone--a promise he conveniently forgot the
moment the prestigious Captain's Badge was pinned to his shirt.

When Rashid Sir, the strict but fair class teacher, handed over the responsibility, his
instructions were crystal clear:

        "Kuddus, you are the 1st Captain. But remember, this is a democracy. You must
        consult your 2nd Captain (Biltu) and 3rd Captain (Miltu) before making any major
        decisions."

Kuddus nodded obediently, but in his head, he was already crowning himself the Emperor of
Section B.

The Reign of Terror

Since taking power, Kodu Kuddus has turned the class into his personal dictatorship,
completely ignoring Biltu and Miltu. His daily atrocities include:

          The Doomsday Syllabus: Scheduling Class Tests on the day of major cricket finals.
         For a simple 10-mark test, he assigns Chapters 1 to 7, including the writer's biography,
         the index, and the barcode on the back cover.
          The Selfish Sports Agenda: Using his veto power during PT period to arrange
         "Ludu Tournaments" because he gasps for air after running for 30 seconds.
          The Great Tiffin Heist: Enacting a 20% "Quality Control Tax" on highly desirable
         tiffins like fried rice or sandwiches.
          The Human Shield Seat Plan: Forcing the tallest boys to sit in the front row to
         perfectly block the teacher's line of sight, allowing Kuddus to sleep peacefully in the
         third row.
          The Washroom Toll: Charging a 2-Taka bribe disguised as the "Class Welfare Fund"
         for anyone needing a washroom break during free periods.

The Loophole

The general students are exhausted, and Biltu and Miltu are incredibly frustrated. According
to the school's ancient rulebook, captains cannot officially file complaints against other
captains.

However, a critical loophole exists: If the class teacher receives legitimate, anonymous
complaints about the 1st Captain exactly three times from the general students, the captain
gets two warnings, and on the third strike, Kuddus will be impeached immediately.

Because the students are too terrified of Kuddus's retaliation, Biltu and Miltu need a
technological revolution. They need you to build the "Anti-Kuddus Protocol"--a system to
organize the students, track Kuddus's tyranny, and safely deliver the 3 strikes to Rashid Sir.

The Hackathon Missions

Participants can implement these features as standalone modules or combine them into a
single ecosystem. Teams will be evaluated based on how far they progress from the baseline
requirements into the advanced engineering challenges.

Mission 1: The Anonymous Whistleblower (Strike Generator)

Build a secure, anonymous portal where students can log Kuddus's daily atrocities.

          Baseline Requirements:
                    Create a secure login interface where students authenticate using a secret
                   class roll-number mapping.
                    A submission form allowing students to log complaints with categories (e.g.,
                   Tiffin Theft, Bribes, Syllabus Bloat) and a text description.
                    A central dashboard featuring a giant, dynamic visual progress bar showing:
                   Warnings: X/3. Strikes left until Impeachment!.

          Advanced Engineering:
                    Absolute Anonymity Pipeline: Implement cryptographic masking or a
                   zero-knowledge data pipeline ensuring that even if the database is leaked or
                   accessed by Kuddus, individual roll numbers cannot be reverse-engineered or
                   linked to specific complaints.
                    Evidence Metadata Stripping: Support photo evidence uploads (e.g.,
                   catching Kuddus mid-heist) but automatically strip EXIF metadata (timestamps,
                   camera signatures) on upload to protect the whistleblower's identity.

Mission 2: The Anti-Camouflage Seat Planner

Kuddus forces the tallest boys to sit in the front row so he can sleep in the back unseen. You
need to build an algorithmic seating planner with a grid UI.

          Baseline Requirements:
                    Create an interface to input a list of student records containing Name, Roll
                   Number, and Height (in cm).
                    Render a static N x M classroom grid UI representing desks.
                    A basic sorting mechanism that places students in ascending order of height
                   from the front row to the back row.

          Advanced Engineering:
                    Line-of-Sight Optimization Algorithm: Create a seating program that
                   calculates the best desk positions. It should ensure the teacher has a clear view
                   of Kuddus's desk, accounting for the teacher's position and the height of
                   students sitting in front.

                    Dynamic Constraints Handling: Handle edge cases such as students with
                   vision/hearing impairments who must sit in the front rows regardless of height,
                   or row/column constraints of the physical classroom structure (e.g., center
                   aisles).

Mission 3: The Syllabus Negotiator (AI Integration)

When Kuddus announces a monstrous syllabus, students panic. Build a tool that parses his
ridiculous demands down to the essentials.

          Baseline Requirements:
                    An input field where students can paste Kuddus's long-form, terrifying
                   syllabus statements.
                    Integration with a LLM API (like OpenAI or Gemini) using a basic system
                   prompt to summarize the text into a clean bulleted list of topics.

          Advanced Engineering:
                    Contextual RAG (Retrieval-Augmented Generation): Inject actual textbook
                   chapter structures or past class questions into the AI context window. The
                   system must cross-reference Kuddus's text against the official curriculum to
                   filter out non-examinable garbage (like "the barcode on the back cover" or "the
                   writer's biography").
                    Smart Study Plan Generator: Output a highly structured, time-blocked
                   JSON format that maps the filtered topics into a personalized, bite-sized study
                   countdown schedule leading up to the test day.

Mission 4: The Corrupt Economy & Tiffin Ledger

Track the illicit financial and caloric wealth Kuddus is accumulating through the 2-Taka
washroom tax and the 20% tiffin extortion.

          Baseline Requirements:
                    A digital ledger system where students anonymously log instances of forced
                   payments (2 Taka) or stolen food items.
                    Standard data visualization showing total cash extorted and total food items
                   collected over time.

          Advanced Engineering:
                    The Caloric vs. Kinetic Disparity Engine: Implement a tracking model that
                   calculates Kuddus's estimated caloric intake from the stolen food database
                   versus his active energy expenditure (modeled mathematically as zero due to
                   his indoor Ludu lifestyle).
                    Projected Weaponry Conversions: Convert his total extorted cash reserves
                   in real time into relatable metrics, dynamically displaying how many
                   international-standard cricket bats or packets of premium jhalmuri he could
                   illegally fund with his corrupt capital.

Mission 5: The SOS Rescue Flare

When Kuddus corners a student in the school corridors to demand a bribe or steal a
sandwich, the student needs immediate backup.

          Baseline Requirements:
                    A prominent, mobile-friendly "SOS" button on the UI.
                    When clicked, the user selects their current location from a hardcoded
                   dropdown menu (e.g., Library, Playground, Corridor, Classroom, Canteen).
                    Updates an active dashboard view for the 2nd and 3rd Captains showing the
                   distress signal.

          Advanced Engineering:
                    Real-Time Event Broadcast: Implement a low-latency socket layer (e.g.,
                   WebSockets or gRPC) or a robust Web Push Notification system that instantly
                   flashes a critical alert window onto the active screens of Biltu and Miltu's
                   devices within milliseconds of the button press, bypassing standard page
                   refreshes.
                    Simulated Network Resilience: - Implement a caching system that stores
                   SOS alerts while offline and automatically syncs them to the server as soon as
                   the network is active.

Mission 6: The Kuddus Fact-Checker

Kuddus frequently makes up fake school rules to maintain power (e.g., "The Headmaster said
1st Captains don't have to do homework"), and students need to debunk his lies instantly.

          Baseline Requirements:
                    A search input interface where students can type a claim made by Kuddus.
                    A basic string-matching system that queries a pre-seeded database table of
                   official school guidelines and returns matching rules.

          Advanced Engineering:
                    Semantic Fact-Checking Engine: Utilize embedding models to perform a
                   semantic search against an unstructured document layout representing the
                   "Official School Rulebook".
                    Confidence Scoring: The system must process the natural language claim,
                   locate the relevant regulatory section, and output a definitive, styled validation
                   card containing:
                              A bold status: [TRUE] or [FALSE]
                              A confidence score metric
                              An exact quote of the real rule to debunk the claim on the spot.
